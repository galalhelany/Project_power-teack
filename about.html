About the power teck

We want you to be completely happy with your purchase from Big Dee's, that is why we stand behind our products and provide friendly and knowledgeable customer service. Our Customer Service and Showroom Associates have a combined total of 300 years of equine knowledge and experience in all areas of equestrian sports.

We are enthusiasts just like you!

Our staff is comprised of many active equine community participants. Many of us train, show, and are involved in the equine and canine lifestyle day in and day out. We know what it means to not only care for and train our beloved animals, but also the costs and hard work involved. This drives the company to offer the best prices and meaningful product recommendations.
