The History of power teck

Founded in 1976 by Edwyn Chet power teck. We started off selling standardbred racing equipment out of a small building in Northfield, Ohio. By offering our customers the products and equipment they needed and at the most affordable prices, Big Dee's quickly started to grow. The company expanded its product lines to

We eventually out grew the first store and moved to Walton Hills, then Oakwood, and with each move, the store and company grew, until finally we found a building that would allow us to continue growing for several years. In the late 90's, Big Dee's settled in Streetsboro, OH. Less than 10 Years later, we remodeled and created an 8000+ sq.ft. showroom offering over 25,000 different equine and canine products and supplements! One final move has kept us in Streetsboro just around the corner from our former location. Our store is bigger and better than ever! The special relationship between a horse and rider embodies the essence of riding sports. Providing exceptional products for the rider to strengthen this bond has always been our driving motivation. We continue to spread the passion for riding according to our motto: It's a Lifestyle.
